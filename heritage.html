Our Heritage

A Legacy of Quality

At King Wagyu Namibia, we take pride in our heritage. As a small family farm focused on producing top-quality Wagyu beef, our commitment to excellence is rooted in tradition. We breed fullblood Japanese Black Wagyu, ensuring the highest marbling for extraordinary flavor. Our closed farming system guarantees a consistent and premium product, from breeding to feedlot. We look forward to sharing our passion with the world.
