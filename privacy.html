Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Who we are

King Wagyu is a Wagyu breeding, embryo, and export operation based in Namibia. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information submitted through this website.

This website is operated by King Wagyu. Enquiries or concerns regarding this policy may be directed to us using the contact details at the bottom of this page.

Information we collect

We collect information only when you voluntarily submit it through one of our enquiry forms. This may include:

  • Your name and email address
  • Your farm, company, or organisation name
  • Your country or region
  • The nature and context of your enquiry
  • Any additional information you choose to provide in open text fields

We do not use tracking cookies, advertising pixels, or any third-party analytics tools on this website. We do not collect data passively.

How we use your information

Information submitted through our enquiry forms is used solely to:

  • Review and assess your enquiry
  • Respond to you directly where there is operational relevance
  • Route your enquiry to the correct person within King Wagyu

We do not use your information for marketing purposes, we do not add you to mailing lists without your explicit agreement, and we do not share your information with third parties for commercial purposes.

How your data is stored

Enquiry submissions are processed through Formspree, a secure form handling service. Formspree stores submissions on secured infrastructure and complies with standard data protection practices. You can review Formspree's own privacy policy at formspree.io.

King Wagyu does not operate its own database of visitor or enquirer data. Information submitted is received by email and managed internally. We retain enquiry information only for as long as it is operationally relevant.

Your rights

You have the right to request access to any personal information we hold about you, to request its correction, or to request its deletion. To make any such request, contact us directly using the details below.

Because we do not operate a centralised database, such requests are handled on a direct, case-by-case basis and are typically resolved promptly.

External links

This website may contain links to external sites. King Wagyu is not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those sites. This policy applies only to kingwagyu.com and its pages.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. We recommend checking this page periodically if you have submitted an enquiry and have concerns about how your information is handled.

Privacy Contact

For any privacy-related questions, requests, or concerns, contact King Wagyu directly through our enquiry system. Mark your enquiry clearly as a privacy matter and it will be handled directly and promptly.
